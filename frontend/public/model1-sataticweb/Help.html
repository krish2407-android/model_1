Help & FAQ

Silver Fashion — shopping, orders, payment & account

At checkout you can pay using UPI, Debit/Credit Card, or Cash on Delivery (COD). After placing your order, you will see a payment success confirmation on the payment page.

Open the product detail page and select your size (XS, S, M, L, XL, or XXL) before adding to cart or buying. You can also change the size from your cart using the size dropdown on each item.

Standard delivery is ₹99 per order. Orders above ₹999 qualify for free delivery. We offer easy returns within 7 days on eligible products. For help, visit our Contact page.

Click Register, fill in your name, email, and password, and sign up. Use the same email and password on the Login page. After login, open My Profile from the menu to see your details or logout anytime.

Shop from Men, Women, Baby Girl, or Baby Boy collections. Review your cart, update quantity or size, then click Proceed To Checkout. Enter delivery details, choose a payment method, and complete your order.
